Hi, ShapeMates!

ShapeSolver is the brainchild of a dynamic duo, Faturrohman Fairuz Zaki and Firdy Dwi Aryani, who embarked on this journey to simplify the world of geometry and mathematics. Launched on September 28, 2023, this web application was born out of a shared passion for making complex calculations a breeze. Fueled by their enthusiasm for education and problem-solving, they set out to create a user-friendly platform that helps users calculate the area and volume of various geometric shapes effortlessly.

Their vision for ShapeSolver extends beyond its launch date; they hope to empower students, educators, and anyone who needs to crunch numbers with an accessible tool that fosters a deeper understanding of geometry. With continuous improvements and a commitment to user feedback, ShapeSolver aims to become the go-to resource for geometry enthusiasts of all ages, making mathematical concepts not only comprehensible but also enjoyable. So, whether you're a student grappling with homework or a professional seeking quick solutions, ShapeSolver is here to simplify the world of shapes and volumes for you.
